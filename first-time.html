First time skydiving?

If you are in Colorado or Wyoming, this is the perfect place for you to come skydive. We are located just outside of Greeley Colorado, not to far from Cheyenne, Fort Collins, Windsor, Longmont, Boulder, or Denver. If you come do a tandem jump with us you will have anazing views of the Rocky Mountains, and the endless plains to the east. If you love the experience (and we know you will), stick around and take the course to become a licensed experienced jumper!

FAQs

Is skydiving safe?

Skydiving is considered an extreme sport. However statistically speaking, skydiving is safer than xyz. Millions of skydives are performed safely each year. At Rocky Mountain Skydiving, we take safety very seriously. Our aircraft are maintained every XXXXX, our tandem equipment is examined by licensed riggers ...... and our tandem instructors are licensed by the USPA and have XXX hours of training and XXX jumps. In addition, all of our tandem and student gear are eqiped with an AAD (Automatic Activation Device) and in the unlikely event that the instructor or student is unable to deploy the main parachute, a reserve parachute will automatically deploy.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothes. Ideally pants or shorts and a t-shirt, depending on the weather. Please wear sneakers, no open toe shoes or sandals. Rocky Mountain Skydiving will provide you with XXXX on the jump.

Will it hurt when the parachute opens?

Our parachutes are packed by experienced/licensed riggers/packers to ensure it will open gently.

Will we land hard?

Our tandem instructors are trained and experienced in making soft and comfortable landings. Just be sure to listen to your tandem instructior when he/she gives you instruction on what to do during landing.

Can I bring a camera on the jump?

Feel free to take as many photos as you like while on the ground, but for safety reasons you cannot bring a camera on the jump. If you would like photos or video footage while falling from xx,xxx ft, please consider one of our professional photo/video packages!

How long will I be there?

Typically, jumps can take from 2 to 3 hours. However, depending on weather conditions, you should plan to spend at least half a day to a full day at the dropzone.

Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are not required but they are recommended as wait times may be longer if you are a walk-in.
