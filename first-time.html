Tandem Skydiving

First time skydiving?

If you are in Colorado or Wyoming, this is the perfect place for you to come skydive. We are located just outside of Greeley Colorado, not to far from Cheyenne, Fort Collins, Windsor, Longmont, Boulder, or Denver. If you come do a tandem jump with us you will have amazing views of the Rocky Mountains, and the endless plains to the east. If you love the experience (and we know you will), stick around and take the course to become a licensed experienced jumper!

Things to know:

  • You must be at least 18 years old to skydive.
  • You must bring a government issued photo ID.
  • You must weigh less than 225 pounds in street clothing.
  • We accept cash, check, visa or mastercard as payment.
  • Due to unforseen weather delays, please be prepared to spend a few hours at the drop-zone. Don't worry, our fun jumpers will keep you pleanty entertained!
  • You must sign a legal waver.
  • There is a $50 deposit required. You must call to cancel at least 48 hours in advance to have the deposit refunded.
  • There is a 10% credit card processing fee.

FAQs

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothes. Ideally pants or shorts and a t-shirt, depending on the weather. Please wear sneakers, no open toe shoes or sandals.

Will it hurt when the parachute opens?

Our parachutes are packed by experienced packers to ensure it will open gently.

Will we land hard?

Our tandem instructors are trained and experienced in making soft and comfortable landings. Just be sure to listen to your tandem instructior when he/she gives you instruction on what to do during landing.

Can I bring a camera on the jump?

Feel free to take as many photos as you like while on the ground, but for safety reasons you cannot bring a camera on the jump. If you would like photos or video footage, please consider one of our professional photo/video packages!

How long will I be there?

Typically, jumps can take from 2 to 3 hours. However, depending on weather conditions, you should plan to spend at least half a day to a full day at the dropzone.

Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are not required but they are recommended as wait times may be longer if you are a walk-in.
