Experienced Skydivers

About Us

We are a fun and friendly Cessna dropzone just outside of Greeley Colorado. We hope you will come visit and make Rocky Mountain Skydiving your home DZ!

Drop Zone Rules

  • You must be a USPA licensed skydiver.
  • AAD required for C license and lower.
  • No dogs allowed at the airport.
  • No smoking on the dropzone.

Camping

We have camping spots on the airport with beautiful views of the mountains.

Hotels

  • Cobblestone Inn and Suites
  • Rodeway Inn

Food!

We occasionally have vendors during events or busy days. There are also a lot of great restaurants in nearby Kersey and Greeley.

  • Kersey Pizza
  • The New Kersey Inn - 106 Hill St, Kersey, CO 80644. (970) 352-2676

Prices
